FAQ, Tips

Please find a set of tips (e.g, food requirements) and questions below. Do let us know if you have other questions or concerns.

QUESTION

How I/we let you know that I/we will be there?

ANSWER

It would be really convenient if you could contact us asap to let us know if you are planning on coming. Nearer the time, there will be a formal invitation. Please RSVP by the 15th of July.

QUESTION

Are you vegetarian or vegan? Do you have a food allergy?

ANSWER

Please let us know when you RSVP, so we can take your preference into account.

QUESTION

Are there facilities for children?

ANSWER

Please let us know if your children are accompanying you. We will provide meal options and organize a children's corner.

QUESTION

Is there parking for the ceremony or reception?

ANSWER

Limited parking is available both at the church, for the ceremony, as well as at the Cantacuzino Castle (about 20 cars).

QUESTION

Are shops open on Sunday?

ANSWER

Almost all shops are open every day (including Sunday) and accept card payments. The currency is the Romanian leu (RON).

QUESTION

Is there a taxi app?

ANSWER

Yes: CleverTaxi. Uber is also available.
